About SDN

The Superhero Dispatch Network (SDN) is a company that employs and dispatches superheroes around Los Angeles. With over 300 heroes in its ranks, SDN provides rapid response to any crisis. Subscribers—including government agencies, corporations, and private citizens—can request assistance 24/7. SDN's commitment to excellence and heroism makes it the premier choice for protection and crisis management in Southern California.
